## 👋 About Me

Hello! I am **Guilherme Sassaki**, an Electrical Engineer, PhD in Chemical Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence specialist with experience in data science, machine learning, computer vision, generative AI, and intelligent automation.

I currently work on the development of enterprise AI solutions, including:

* Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems
* AI agents and LLM-based applications
* Vector and hybrid search
* API and cloud-service integrations
* Document processing and knowledge retrieval pipelines
* Machine learning and computer vision solutions
* AI and Power Platform governance

My current technical focus includes **Python, Azure AI Foundry, Azure AI Search, Copilot Studio, Power Platform, FastAPI, LangChain, Docker, PyTorch, TensorFlow, OpenCV**, and cloud-based AI architectures.

My doctoral research focused on the tuning of fractional-order PID controllers for time-delay systems using metaheuristic optimization algorithms, particularly **Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)** and **Differential Evolution (DE)**. This work strengthened my background in mathematical modeling, optimization, control systems, and applied research.

I also completed an Artificial Intelligence residency at the **SENAI Londrina AI HUB**, in partnership with **UTFPR**, where I worked on real-world industry projects involving machine learning, data analysis, Python development, AI model implementation, and computer vision.
